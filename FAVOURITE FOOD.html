CHILLI PRAWN AND SPAGHETTI

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g dried spaghetti

  • 2tsp extra virgin oil

  • 2 garlic cloves , thinly sliced

  • pinch dried chilli flakes

  • 10(350g) mideim green prowns ,peeled (tails intact),deveined

  • 3 medium tomatoes, deseeded, finely chopped

  • 1tbsp chopped fresh continental parsely leaves

STEPS TO FOLLOW


  1. cook pasta in a large saucepan of boiling , salted water following packet directions, until tender.

  2. drain ,reseriving 1/4 cup cooking liquid

  3. meanwhile heat oil in a large frying panover medium heat

  4. add garlic and chilli

  5. cook for one minute until fragrant

  6. add prowns

  7. cook stiring for two or three minutes or until pink and cooked through

  8. cook for two minutes until heated through

  9. season with salt and pepper

  10. stir through parsely and serve

TIPS

serve while hot
